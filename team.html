Dan Bode

CEO

Dan recently founded Bodeco to build out a consulting practice targeted at large scale infrastructure automation solutions. He is currently focused on building deployment solutions for large scale OpenStack environments.

Previously, he did a four year stint at PuppetLabs, where he helped grow the company as one of the earliest employees.

Prior to Puppet Labs, Dan held a wide range of technical positions supporting scientific researchers in HPC environments.

Nan Liu

Co-founder

Nan is the co-founder of Bodeco where he helps customers design and implement continuous delivery systems using Puppet and other open source tools.

He has extensive experience using Puppet to automate data center infrastructure services, and co-authored O'Reilly's Puppet Types and Providers based on that experience.

He previously worked at VMware, where he helped build a continuous delivery system for their public cloud offering vCloud Air. Nan is also a Puppet Labs alumni who implemented numerous third party integrations as a founding member of the professional services team.
